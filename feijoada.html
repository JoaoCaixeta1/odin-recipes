Feijoada

Method of Preparation

Feijoada is a typical Brazilian food but isn't found throughout the country. That's because all Brazilian states have your own typical and mainfood. Althout Feijoada can be found in many states in the country, because your flavor is impressively good.

Many people do Feijoada in different ways, but must people do cooking all pig parts, like foot, ears, meat, rib, bacon and pepperoni.

First, you will take a pressure cooker and fry chopped onion and garlic, then all pig parts with peperoni. Then put the black bean in the same pan and mixture. When you realize that is in a good consistency, put water almost to the top and bay leaves.

After taking pressure, leave it for arount 30 minutes. After all, open the pressure cooker and take some Feijoada in a separate pan to cook a little bit more and reduce the broth.

Ingredients:

  • All pig parts
  • Pepperoni
  • Oil
  • Black bean
  • Bay leaves
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Water

Ingredients Order:

  1. Oil
  2. Garlic + Onion
  3. All pig parts + Pepperoni
  4. Black bean
  5. Water
  6. Bay leaves
