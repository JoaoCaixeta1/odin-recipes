Sushi

Method of Preparation

Sushi is a typical Japanese food that has gain potencial force in countrys like Brazil. It's delicious and don't have many ingredients to use, but the process is a little bit hard to do, mainly if you are beginner.

Firstly you will do the Japanese rice named Gohan , then you will make slices of salmon or whathever fish you prefer. After that, you will take the seaweed and put the Gohan in the center, spreading it well and leaving it uniform to place the salmon slices on top with creamcheese.

Finishing, it's just take a very good knife to cut the Sushi and put in a plate to serve whoever you want.

Ingredients:

  • Salmon or anyother fish
  • Seaweed
  • Gohan
  • Creamcheese

Ingredients Order:

  1. Gohan
  2. Salmon
  3. Seaweed
  4. Creamcheese
